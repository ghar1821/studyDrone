How does it work?




The notes you need, when you need them

You're not alone at Usyd and your studying should reflect that. Studydrone lets you find and download notes for all your courses, uploaded by your talented peers and sorted by relevancy and quality to suit your courses. And when you're ready, spread the love by uploading your own notes in turn.


Glory and riches await, just for you

The more you participate on the site, the more points you're awarded. You earn points just for using the site, like when you rate someone elses notes or when you act in a group. Users who consistently upload high-quality notes are rewarded fastest, so you can be sure that your efforts won't go to waste!


Getting your kebab fix has never been so easy

We've always thought the walk to Wentworth was overrated. Instead, have Uni Bros come to you with their delicious range. Order a kebab from them and they'll deliver straight to the engineering district within 20 minutes. And because we like you so much, we've talked them into not charging for delivery.


Redeem your points for a free lunch

We promised all those points would come in handy, and we've definitely delivered. Your points can now be cashed in on our website in exchange for free food from Uni Bros! Eventually we'll be partnering with other campus retail outlets but for now, why not enjoy some free kebabs, on us?
